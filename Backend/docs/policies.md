# DRINKOO Policies

## Returns
DRINKOO accepts returns within 14 days of delivery for unopened sealed product. Opened beverages cannot be returned for food safety reasons. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5 business days of receipt of the returned item.

## Damaged orders
Damaged orders must be reported within 48 hours of delivery via the My Orders page. Submit clear photos. DRINKOO will replace the damaged units free of charge or issue a refund within 5 business days.

## Shipping
DRINKOO ships across continental USA in 2 to 4 business days, Canada in 3 to 6 business days, and selected EU countries in 5 to 8 business days. Bulk orders may take 5 to 7 business days regardless of zone.

## Allergens
DRINKOO products are produced in facilities that also handle cocoa and milk solids. Products in our cold brew mocha line contain cocoa and milk and should be avoided by people with cocoa or dairy allergies. All other DRINKOO products are free of common allergens, but the label always shows the latest information.

## Privacy
DRINKOO never sells customer data. Only data required to fulfill the order, deliver shipments, and provide support is collected and retained for the legally required period.

## Bulk pricing
Customers ordering 24 or more units of any bulk-eligible product automatically receive a 20 percent discount via the BULK20 code at checkout.
