# DRINKOO Frequently Asked Questions

## What is DRINKOO?
DRINKOO is a beverage company that makes sparkling and still drinks across citrus, berry, herbal, tea, water, kids, sports, and cold-brew coffee categories. Every DRINKOO product is labeled with sugar content per 100 ml and ingredient sourcing.

## Where can I see nutrition information?
Every DRINKOO product page shows sugar grams per 100 ml, calories per 100 ml, and ingredient percentages. The chatbot can answer questions about specific products by name.

## Do you sell bulk?
Yes. Bulk-eligible products are tagged on the catalog and qualify for the BULK20 promotion when you order 24 or more units.

## What about kids?
The Kiddo line is targeted at kids with no artificial colors and lower price points. See products Kiddo Apple and Kiddo Mango.

## Are DRINKOO drinks vegan?
Most DRINKOO products are vegan-friendly. The Cold Brew Mocha line contains milk solids and is not vegan. The allergen support article lists products with cocoa and milk.

## Can I ask in natural language about your catalog?
Yes. The DRINKOO chatbot can answer questions in plain English about products, ingredients, promotions, and policies. It uses retrieval over our internal catalog and support knowledge base.
